\subsection{Rotation matrix 2D}
Rotation of point $(x, y)$ through an angle $\alpha$ in counterclockwise direction in 2D.

$$
\begin{pmatrix}
\cos \alpha & -\sin \alpha \\
\sin \alpha & \cos \alpha
\end{pmatrix}
\cdot
\begin{pmatrix}
x \\
y
\end{pmatrix}
=
\begin{pmatrix}
x' \\
y'
\end{pmatrix}
$$
